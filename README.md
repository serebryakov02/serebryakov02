## About Me

I work full time as a software engineer at [GeoSoftware](https://www.geosoftware.com/), building and maintaining C++/Qt desktop software with CMake-based build systems, careful debugging, and clean desktop application architecture.

I have about 3 years of professional software engineering experience and around 6 years of programming experience overall. I am a self-taught programmer with a bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering, and I keep learning by building practical projects across C++, Python, AI automation, and developer tooling.

In my free time, I like contributing to open-source projects and helping other people where I can, especially around desktop UX, debugging, and improvements that make software easier to use and maintain.
